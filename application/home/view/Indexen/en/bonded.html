Bonded Imports

Imported B2B2C services, mainly for cross-border e-commerce platform, provide integrated services for transportation from abroad, domestic bonded areas for stocking, bonded warehousing, and customs clearance, sorting, packaging and delivery directly from bonded warehouses when orders are generated.

Imported B2B2C services are carried out in the form of “in bulk batches, distributed”; the whole batch of imported goods is filed with the customs through the Tianzhong International Integrated Service Platform and declared to enter the Customs Bonded Zone; “Distributed” is cross-border electricity. The merchants will send the orders generated after the personal online shopping to the Tianzhong service platform, and the platform will immediately declare to the customs to realize the automatic electronic customs clearance. After the customs clearance is successful, the bonded warehouse will be shipped out of the order and dispatched.
